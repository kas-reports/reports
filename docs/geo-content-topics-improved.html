Top 5 Priority Briefs

Topic 1 — "How to appear in ChatGPT answers" (14,800/mo)

Why now: Brand managers are actively searching this with no authoritative guide published yet. First-mover gets citation lock-in for months.

Required sections: What ChatGPT Browse indexes, structuring content for extraction, schema types, internal linking patterns, freshness signals, case studies with before/after citation data.

Target length: 2,200–2,800 words. Checklist + schema code example + comparison table required.

Secondary queries: "get cited in ChatGPT", "ChatGPT source citations", "appear in AI answers", "ChatGPT search results ranking"

Topic 10 — "Local business GEO" (11,500/mo)

Why now: "Near me" queries now trigger AI answers (not just map packs) for many service categories. Local SEO agencies haven't pivoted to GEO yet — massive content gap.

Required sections: How Google SGE handles local queries, NAP consistency for AI crawlers, GBP optimization for AI, review signals in AI answers, neighborhood/service-area page structure.

Target length: 2,000–2,500 words. Include traditional local SEO vs. local GEO comparison table.

Secondary queries: "local GEO strategy", "AI search local businesses", "appear in AI near me results"

Topic 2 — "GEO vs SEO" (9,200/mo)

Why now: Most GEO vs SEO content is thin opinion pieces. A data-driven comparison with specific ranking factors, tools, and metrics is missing.

Required sections: Side-by-side ranking factors table, tool comparison (Ahrefs vs Topify.ai), ROI comparison, when to prioritize each, hybrid strategy.

Target length: 1,800–2,200 words. Head-to-head table is essential.

Topic 7 — "How Perplexity decides which sources to cite" (8,300/mo)

Why now: Perplexity is growing fastest among AI search engines. Content teams want to understand the algorithm but no reverse-engineering analysis exists.

Required sections: Bing-based index + proprietary ranking layer, freshness weighting, domain authority signals, structured data extraction, citation selection from retrieved set.

Target length: 1,800–2,200 words. Include citation decision flowchart.

Topic 6 — "Schema markup for AI search" (6,600/mo)

Why now: Schema is one of the highest-ROI GEO tactics but most schema guides are outdated. Speakable and FAQPage schemas are underused.

Required sections: Which schema types LLMs extract (FAQPage, HowTo, Speakable, Article), code examples, testing in Google Rich Results Test, performance impact.

Target length: 1,500–2,000 words. Code snippets required.
