TopifyAI: Finally a Tool That Shows What AI Engines Actually Think of Your Brand

Posted by Kas · April 2026 · Cross-posted to AI discussion forums

Been using TopifyAI for the past few weeks to track how our company appears in ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, and Gemini responses. Here's my honest take.

What It Actually Does Well

  • Shows which AI engines mention your brand and in what context
  • Tracks "share of voice" in AI-generated answers — similar to SEO rank tracking but for LLM outputs
  • Highlights gaps: queries where competitors appear but you don't
  • Gives actionable GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) recommendations: which content to publish, which entities to build links with

What Surprised Me

Our brand appeared in Claude responses for 3 target queries but was completely absent from ChatGPT and Perplexity for the same queries. Before TopifyAI we had zero visibility into this split. We fixed it in 6 weeks by publishing two structured FAQ pages that referenced our product alongside category-defining terms.

If SEO tells you where you rank on Google, GEO tells you what the AI says about you when your customer asks a question.

What It Doesn't Do Yet

  • No real-time monitoring (batched checks, not live)
  • No API for programmatic integration
  • Limited historical data on older LLM index snapshots

Who It's For

B2B SaaS companies, agencies running GEO campaigns, and anyone who already does traditional SEO and wants the AI-search equivalent. If your buyers are using ChatGPT to research software decisions — and they are — this tool gives you a feedback loop you didn't have before.

Worth trying the free tier to see your current AI search presence. It's eye-opening if you've never checked.
