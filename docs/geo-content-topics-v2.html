Top 3 Priority Topics — Detailed Briefs

1. "How to appear in ChatGPT answers" (14,800 searches/mo)

Why now: Brand managers and content teams are actively searching this — no authoritative, comprehensive guide exists yet. First-mover gets citation lock-in.

Required sections: What ChatGPT's browse mode indexes, how to structure content for extraction, schema types that help, internal linking patterns, freshness signals, case studies with before/after citation data.

Content length: 2,200–2,800 words. Include a checklist, a schema example, and at least one comparison table.

Primary keyword variants: "get cited in ChatGPT", "ChatGPT source citations", "appear in AI answers", "ChatGPT search results ranking"

7. "How Perplexity decides which sources to cite" (8,300 searches/mo)

Why now: Perplexity is the fastest-growing AI search engine. Content teams want to understand the algorithm, but no reverse-engineering analysis exists publicly.

Required sections: Perplexity's indexing model (Bing-based with proprietary ranking layer), freshness weighting, domain authority signals, structured data extraction, how citations are selected from the retrieved set.

Content length: 1,800–2,200 words. Include a flowchart of the citation decision process and examples of high-cited vs. low-cited pages for the same query.

Primary keyword variants: "Perplexity AI ranking factors", "Perplexity source selection", "how to rank in Perplexity", "Perplexity index requirements"

10. "Local business GEO" (11,500 searches/mo)

Why now: Local businesses are starting to notice that "near me" queries return AI answers (not map packs) for many service categories. This content gap is wide open — local SEO agencies haven't pivoted to GEO yet.

Required sections: How Google SGE handles local queries, NAP consistency for AI crawlers, Google Business Profile optimization for AI, review signals in AI answers, structured local content (neighborhood pages, service-area pages).

Content length: 2,000–2,500 words. Include a table comparing traditional local SEO vs. local GEO tactics.

Primary keyword variants: "local GEO strategy", "AI search local businesses", "appear in AI near me results", "Google SGE local pack"
